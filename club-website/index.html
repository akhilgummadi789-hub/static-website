A community built to empower.

The C3 is a student-run community for anyone interested in technology. We organise coding sessions, hands-on workshops, and exciting hackathons where students can learn new skills, build cool projects, and collaborate with friends from different branches.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer, there's a place for you here. Our goal is to make cloud and modern tech accessible, practical, and fun for every student on campus.
